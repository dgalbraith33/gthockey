About the Coaches
Bob Cernich - Head Coach
Coach Cernich has been involved in the Atlanta youth hockey community for many years and has successfully coached several youth team to the national tournament. He joined the coaching staff at the start of the 2015-16 season looking to usher in a new era of GT Hockey more focused on team play than individual accomplishment.
Paul Gattung - Manager
Coach Gattung has been involved with GT Hockey ever since his son Christian played on the team many years ago. Gattung has been instrumental in growing and promoting the team on and off the ice. And can be seen as the team's liaison at Regional and National ACHA events. He also serves as the unofficial contact for alumni events and relations.
Joe Kraft - Assistant Coach
Joe Kraft arrived on the scene recently to serve as an Assistant Coach and has been very helpful both on and off the ice. When not on the Georgia Tech bench Coach Kraft is also involved in the Atlanta youth hockey community, coaching a PeeWee team and grooming the next generation of Tech hockey players.
Maxx Lucas - Assistant/Goaltender Coach
Maxx joined the coaching staff for the 2015-16 season after completing his Master's Degree in Aerospace Engineering the previous season and exhausting his ACHA eligibility. Pursuing his PhD at Tech, Maxx now uses his extensive knowledge of the game to help the team's current goaltenders grow and develop. He has also worked to vastly improve the team's pre-season and in-season off-ice training regimen.
