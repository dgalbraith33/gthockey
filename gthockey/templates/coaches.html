About the Coaches
Bob Cernich - Head Coach
Coach Cernich has been involved in the Atlanta youth hockey community for many years and has successfully coached several youth team to the national tournament. He joined the coaching staff at the start of the 2015-16 season looking to usher in a new era of GT Hockey more focused on team play than individual accomplishment.
Paul Gattung - Manager
Coach Gattung has been involved with GT Hockey ever since his son Christian played on the team many years ago. Gattung has been instrumental in growing and promoting the team on and off the ice. And can be seen as the team's liaison at Regional and National ACHA events. He also serves as the unofficial contact for alumni events and relations.
